Chilcoot Fire Burning Near Hallelujah Junction

A 1,021-acre wildfire is burning just north of Hallelujah Junction.

The Chilcoot fire is burning in California about 15 miles west of the California/ Nevada border.

It's currently 90% contained.

Authorities say evacuation orders were lifted for the area south of Highway 70.
